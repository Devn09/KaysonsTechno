Company profile
“Protection begins with understanding the environment, the application and the consequence of failure.”

Kaysons is positioned around one demanding field: electrical equipment intended for hazardous industrial locations.

Our portfolio spans lighting, local control, power connection, panels, field termination, instrument protection and installation accessories. Product selection and configuration are approached through the actual application requirement.

As detailed product photographs, model tables and documents are supplied, this website will become the structured technical access point for the complete Kaysons portfolio.
