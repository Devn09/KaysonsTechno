About us

Application understanding. Industrial execution.

Kaysons Techno Equipments Private Limited designs and manufactures explosion-protected electrical equipment for hazardous and demanding industrial environments from its base in Vitthal Udyognagar, Anand, Gujarat.

01 Company overview

Engineering equipment for environments where details matter.

Kaysons Techno Equipments Private Limited focuses on electrical equipment for hazardous areas, including lighting, local control, motor starting, connection, panel, enclosure, instrument and cable-entry product families.

The company’s approach starts with the application: area classification, gas group, environmental conditions, electrical duty, material, cable entries, components, certification needs and project documentation.
